    Optima Chicago Center Reviews

    200 E Illinois Street is a glass construction (2013), full-amenity rental building in southwest Streeterville.

     

    What occupants love

    Fun pool/deck scene. Solid shower pressure and internet/Wi-Fi connectivity. Well maintained. Expansive gym offering.

     

    What occupants love less

    Some residents believe the surrounding neighborhood can get touristy. Moderate elevator wait times.

     

    Nearest Grocery Store: Whole Foods Market (2-minute walk)

    Nearest Convenience Store: 7-Eleven (6-minute walk)

    Nearest CTA: Red Line - Grand (9-minute walk)
